Bombshell Restage

With the restage of the Bombshell Collection, I worked on emphasizing the peony scent note across store fixtures. I constructed methods to execute the designs and worked through multiple rounds of samples with vendors. I made hands-on mockups and modifications on the samples to land floral arrangements with the Vice President of Visual Merchandising.

Process

This project started as renderings on the computer and then it took a lot of hands-on work to quickly modify flower arrangements in-house to land designs for multiple fixtures. With samples from multiple vendors, I kept track of flowers and cut and pasted them to execute a full look. After multiple rounds of arrangements and production drawings, we landed the final designs.
