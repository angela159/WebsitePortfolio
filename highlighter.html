Product Highlighter

The goal of this project was to design a flexible highlighter that could be used across all categories. It’s meant to be a testing destination but also keeps the overall merchandising presentation elevated and consistent across the different size cabinet fixtures in 800 stores. I also designed different elements as part of the whole package so there were opportunities for the different merchandising strategies.

Process

The fixture was inspired by previous highlighters that I designed that were more simple but ultimately served the same purpose. I worked with the team to see what worked well previously and made sure to keep those functionalities in mind while ideating the new design. After brainstorming potential strategies, I added layers to the design to create a flexible highlighter for all the categories but kept the simple presentation.
