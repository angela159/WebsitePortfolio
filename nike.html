Nike Store Concept

This is a small freestanding Nike store that focuses on Nike’s Air Max sneaker line. The idea started from the concept of the Air Max bubble that made the line so iconic. Looking at the profile of the shoe you were able to see light shine through. No other sneaker did that at the time. The concept started out as finding a way to bring the air bubble into the space but slowly evolved into something more functional and shoppable. Pulling from Nike’s original store aesthetic, I ended up designing something that still held the original concept with the light shining through, but created a less overwhelming shopping experience within a small space.
