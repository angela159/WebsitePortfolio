Pink Beauty

When quarantine started, I took on the responsibility of putting together the virtual space for PINK Beauty. I designed a mock space to resemble a designated PINK Beauty room and pulled together all products and fixtures. I collaborated with the merchandising team to fine-tune details on their end as well. Through rounds of changes and navigating the space, the team was able to continue to present our merchandising strategies to all partners and the leadership team while working from home.
