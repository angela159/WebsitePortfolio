Energy Innovation Center By Tesla

This project is based on a retail space located in Chelsea and designed to educate customers on the range of products and their benefits that Tesla has to offer. Using the existing columns in the space, I was able to create sections for a better flow of the space, separate areas for information about the company, and areas to explore the details about the products themselves.
